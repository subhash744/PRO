FAQ

Common questions, clear answers.

Which AI tools does Prompt Autocomplete work on?
Prompt Autocomplete works on 50+ AI tools — every major AI chatbot, coding assistant, and image generation platform available in Chrome. The list covers all the tools most people already use daily, and new ones are added with every release.
Does saving a prompt once make it available on all AI tools?
Yes. Save a prompt once and it appears in the autocomplete dropdown on every supported AI tool. You do not need to re-save or reconfigure anything when switching between chatbots or assistants.
Where are my prompts stored? Is anything sent to a server?
Your prompts are stored entirely in your browser using Chrome's local storage API. Nothing is sent to any external server. Pro license activation sends only your license key for a one-time validation — no prompt content ever leaves your device.
What are slash commands and how do they work?
Slash commands let you filter prompts by tag in any AI input. If you have tagged prompts as "email", type /email and only those prompts appear. It is a fast keyboard shortcut for people who have prompts across multiple categories.
Is the $6 Pro price a subscription?
No. $6 is a one-time payment. You receive a license key that unlocks unlimited prompts permanently. No monthly billing, no renewal, no account required after activation. The price is planned to increase as more features are added.
What is the prompt history navigator?
It works like terminal command history. Use the arrow keys in any AI input field to cycle through prompts you have sent during that session. Useful for iterating on variations of the same prompt without retyping it from scratch.
